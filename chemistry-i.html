Concept of LCAO approximation & diff. Types of MO's

i) LCAO approximation is a method used in quantum chemistry to construct molecular orbital by combining atomic orbital of individual atom's in a molecule
ii) In LCAO, Atomic orbital like (1s, 2px etc...) from different atoms in a molecule are mathematically combined to form molecular orbital
iii) Each molecular orbital represented as a linear combination (weight sum) of atomic orbital.
iv) The coefficient of this combination are determined by solving the Schrodinger equation for entire molecule.
